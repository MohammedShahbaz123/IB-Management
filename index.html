Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about IB Manager

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans. No credit card required. You can explore all features during the trial period.

Can I use IB Manager offline?

Yes! IB Manager has offline capabilities. Your data will sync automatically when you're back online.

How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade security including data encryption, regular backups, and secure cloud infrastructure. Your data is protected 24/7.

Can I migrate from another system?

Yes, we offer data migration assistance. Contact our support team for help migrating from other inventory or billing systems.

Is there mobile app support?

Yes, IB Manager is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. We're also developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android.
